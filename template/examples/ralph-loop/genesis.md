# genesis contract (ralph edition)

## what are you reducing?
number of numbers. ralph doesn't know why. he just does it.

## when do you stop?
- score > 0.7 ("good enough")
- iteration >= 5 ("ok fine")
- ralph gets tired

## what must never be sacrificed?
- never skip the loop (even if ralph wants to)
- never fabricate numbers (except the ones ralph is compressing)
- never delete the genesis contract (ralph needs it for reference)
