The three mechanisms

A

3.0Ã— training penalty (Sapir-Whorf term)

3Ã— weighted cross-entropy on must-keep tokens during fine-tuning. Penalizes false evictions of critical-syntactic tokens (numbers, paths, error codes) 3Ã— harder than dropping filler words.

B

Post-inference regex override

Surgical safety net at deployment. After the model scores each token, a sliding window checks dropped tokens against MUST_KEEP_RE. Matching tokens are force-kept. Costs ~0.1ms per chunk.

C

Self-labeling loop

Use A+B as an oracle to relabel the training data, then retrain. v4 internalized the override (override_delta=0) in one generation. v8 uses C3 self-distillation with a Qwen2.5-7B teacher.
