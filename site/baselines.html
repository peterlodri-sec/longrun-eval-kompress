Pareto Frontier: The λ-Ablation

The must-keep loss weight λ controls the precision–compression tradeoff. v8's training data held constant; only λ varies. The frontier is monotonic and clean.

Each +1.0 in λ buys ~0.01 heretic precision at the cost of ~10% compression. At λ=10, the model keeps 97.2% of tokens — useless as a compressor despite the best precision score. λ=3 (v8) is the knee of the curve: 0.955 heretic with 15% compression is the optimal tradeoff for deployment.
